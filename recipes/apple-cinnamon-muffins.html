Apple Cinnamon Muffins

The best muffin flavor!

These are my absolute favorite muffins to make on any occasion. Apple and cinnamon are such a wonderful combination. Fruity and spicy!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Apples
  • Cinnamon
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Milk

Directions

  1. Mix the wet ingredients
  2. Add in the dry ingredients
  3. Beat until mixed but still lumpy
  4. fill muffin tin half full
  5. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes
