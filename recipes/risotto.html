Risotto

Lemon Asparagus Risotto in all its glory

This asparagus risotto is flavored with garlic, white wine, lemon, and Parmesan cheese. I like to serve this as a main dish under some steamed halibut or other white fish.

Ingredients

  • Arborio Rice
  • Lemon
  • Asparagus
  • Cheese
  • Broth

Directions

  1. Cook the rice
  2. let it soak in the the broth
  3. cook the asparagus
  4. put the asparagus cheese and lemon in the rice
  5. eat!
