Grilled Cheese

A delicious Grilled Cheese!

Everyone needs to know how to make a classic grilled cheese sandwich. Whether you're a beginner cook or an old pro, you'll come back to this top-rated grilled cheese recipe again and again.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Bread
  • Cheese

Directions

  1. Butter the bread
  2. place bread on han preheated to medium heat
  3. add cheese to bread
  4. add other slice of buttered bread
  5. flip after 3 - 5 minutes
  6. cook for 3 - 5 minutes on other side
